Pleasure to meet you!

I'm Francis. I've never been much of writer, but nonetheless I've always had a passion for stories.

So here's mine.

Both of my parents grew up in the Philippines. I, however, was born and bred in New York City in the heart of Spanish Harlem. As I grew older we moved to a more suburban area in Queens, and that's where I grew to love the quaintness of living in a home rather than a 750-square foot apartment. But nevertheless there was a degree of rich culture and liveliness that I simply missed.

Before I knew it, I packed my bags and move upstate to attend Cornell University in Ithaca, New York. While there, I'm an engineer by day and an avid bubble tea drinker by night. If you've ever stepped foot on the campus, you've probably seen me in Olin Library or Duffield Hall working on a problem set, planning out my schedule, or aimlessly reading New York Times Op-Ed articles. And if you don't see me in one of those places, call the police.

For more specific inquiries about the best restaurants in New York City or the prime bubble tea spots in Ithaca, New York, I'm your guy.
